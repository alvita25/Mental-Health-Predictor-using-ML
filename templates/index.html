Welcome to Chat2Care

Chat2Care is your intelligent mental health assistant powered by AI. Our chatbot listens to your thoughts, recognizes your emotions, and helps assess your mental well-being using machine learning models like XGBoost, LightGBM, Logistic Regression, Naive Bayes, and Random Forest.
